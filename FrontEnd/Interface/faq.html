Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I search for a course?
    Use the search bar at the top and type part of the course name, then click Search.
  • What does "Filter by" do?
    It changes whether your search text matches the course Name, Category, or Status.
  • How do I filter by term?
    Select a term from the Term dropdown (Fall, Winter, Spring). Only courses from that term will show.
